ABOUT HACKATHON

Bored of sitting at home with so many ideas buzzing in your head? Let your imagination take wing and give life to your many innovative ideas by turning them into reality! Build it into an extraordinary project and present your ideas to our esteemed panel of judges by participating in IEM-HACKATHON 2021, organized by the Institute of Engineering & Management and stand a chance at winning some very exciting prizes! Beginners are most welcome!

IEM HACKATHON 1.0 is the very first initiative by students of the Institute of Engineering & Management to host a hackathon that will assist in creating a co-working space for participants to brainstorm and create a virtual reality prototype. The participants will try to 'hack' their way by thinking out of the box. We envision developing a workplace where diversified minds and ideas come together under one roof to create solutions.

A competition like this aims at developing something awesome within a limited timeframe infuses work pressure among the participants, testing them beyond their limits. The most valuable part of any hackathon is the learning experience. Hackathons are great arenas for establishing bonds with like-minded individuals, gaining knowledge outside of your comfort zone and learning now people can work together to achieve a common purpose.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What will be the selection criteria?

We will review your Github profile and send you the selection email a day or two before the hackathon starts.

What is the last date of registration?

The deadline for registrations is 27.04.2021.

Do we need to have the entire idea fully working?

The entire idea need not be fully implemented however, the submission should be functional so that it can be reviewed by the judges.

What if I don't have a team for the Hackathon?

Not an issue! Join our Discord Server to meet developer's from across the community and find your team to build your project.
